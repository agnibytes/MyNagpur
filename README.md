# AI-Powered Urban Insights Platform

## Overview

The **AI-Powered Urban Insights Platform** is a prototype Smart City analytics system designed to help city planners and administrators analyze urban data and generate actionable insights. Modern cities produce large amounts of data from sources such as traffic systems, environmental sensors, public transportation networks, and energy grids. However, this data is often fragmented across multiple systems and difficult to interpret.

This platform demonstrates how urban datasets can be integrated, analyzed using artificial intelligence techniques, and visualized through an interactive dashboard to support data-driven urban planning and decision making.

The prototype focuses on analyzing **traffic and environmental data** to identify patterns and predict pollution levels based on traffic density.

---

## Problem Statement

Modern urban infrastructures generate large volumes of data from sensors, connected devices, and digital platforms. This data includes information about traffic flow, air pollution levels, transportation usage, energy consumption, and other city operations.

Despite the availability of this data, it is often stored across different systems and formats, making it difficult for city authorities to analyze and extract meaningful insights. Without effective tools for data integration, analysis, and visualization, decision-makers struggle to transform raw data into actionable information.

This project aims to develop a **Smart City Data Analyzer** that integrates urban datasets and provides AI-driven insights to support efficient urban management and sustainable city planning.

## Objectives

## Key Features

### Data Integration

The platform combines multiple urban datasets including traffic density and air pollution levels. Data is cleaned and processed to ensure consistency and usability.

### Data Analysis

Exploratory data analysis is performed to understand trends and relationships between traffic and environmental conditions.

### AI-Based Prediction

A machine learning model is implemented to predict air pollution levels based on traffic density using regression techniques.

### Interactive Dashboard

A user-friendly dashboard displays key insights through graphs and visualizations, helping users understand urban patterns quickly.

### Urban Insights

The system highlights important findings such as:

* Areas with highest traffic congestion
* Pollution level trends
* Correlation between traffic and pollution
